Here's how I help teams iterate faster with less bugs:

Your tests can be faster

Many teams recognize that because of how quickly unit tests can execute, unit tests should make up the majority of their test suite.1 However, when they attempt to write unit tests for their code, they may not know where to start or their code may in fact be untestable. I help teams identify parts of their code base that are not amenable to unit testing and suggest refactoring methods that will make the code more unit testable.

Your QA team can write functional tests

QA teams can perform important functions, even in a team that has a large automated test suite.2 I can help make your QA team more effective by helping your developers create a testing DSL simple enough for your QA team to use in writing functional tests.

Your Testing Process can be streamlined

Testing can often be a repetitive process. The way your team gets builds to QA, for example, may not be as efficient as it could be. I can help your team identify repettive tasks that are susceptible to automation.
